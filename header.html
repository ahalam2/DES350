About the Park!

Yellowstone National Park became the first national park in the United states in 1872. The park is known for its extensive wildlife and prominent geothermal features, such as the famous “Old Faithful” geyser. Yellowstone National Park spans an area of 3,468.4 square miles, and is located across three states which include Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho. Yellowstone also holds many different lakes, canyons, rivers, and mountains.
